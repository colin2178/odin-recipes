Carnivore Pizza

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Sauce
  • Ham
  • Mushrooms
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef until brown (and spice if desired)
  2. Cook mushrooms in butter (can substitute with olive oil & any spices to taste
  3. Put ground beef in oven container as a base
  4. Pour sauce on top of beef to taste
  5. Layer ham on top of sauce to a desired thickness
  6. Add mushrooms on top of ham spreading evenly
  7. Grade cheese & sprinkle evenly all around on top
  8. Put in oven at 280 for about 30-45 min until cheese is melted & brown to taste

NOTE: I used plain tomato paste for the sauce & added garlic, salt/pepper to the beef and mushrooms
