Chocolate Oatmeal Drop Cookie

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of oats
  • 1 cup of shredded coconut
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 3 tbsp of cocao

Steps

  1. Combine oats & coconut in separate bowl
  2. Combine milk, sugar, butter and cocao in pot & boil for ruffly 3 min
  3. Add vanilla to liquid
  4. Add liquid to dry mix
  5. Stir until well mixed
  6. Spoon out cookies to desired size onto a cookie sheet & put the in freezer

Note: Caution when eating these, they can become addicting!
