Protein Shake
Ingredients
  • 200ml of kefir
  • 2 scoops of iso protein
  • 1 scoop of collagen
  • 1/2 cup of mixed berries
  • Couple of strawberries
  • Banana slice
  • Coffee beans

Steps

  1. Pour kefir into blender with the protein & collagen (mix well)
  2. Add mixed berries & strawberries to liquid and mix well
  3. Once Ingredients are blended pour into glass(s)
  4. Add banana slice and beans
  5. Insert straw

Note: Take time to manually stir the liquid & let out the air from the blending process. Also there may be gases from the fruit released bubbling up.
